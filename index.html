Featured Destination

Deduru Oya, Sri Lanka

A hidden gem nestled in the Kurunegala District

Deduru Oya is a picturesque reservoir located in the verdant landscapes of Sri Lanka. This sprawling body of water, created by the Deduru Oya Dam, serves as a vital source of irrigation and hydroelectric power generation for the region.

Surrounding the reservoir, lush forests and rolling hills provide a serene backdrop for leisurely activities such as fishing, birdwatching, and boat rides. The tranquil ambiance and scenic beauty of Deduru Oya attract both locals and tourists seeking respite from the hustle and bustle of urban life.
