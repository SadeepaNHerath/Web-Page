Deduru Oya, Sri Lanka

Deduru Oya, nestled in the verdant landscapes of Sri Lanka, is a picturesque reservoir located in the Kurunegala District. This sprawling body of water, created by the Deduru Oya Dam, serves as a vital source of irrigation and hydroelectric power generation for the region. Surrounding the reservoir, lush forests and rolling hills provide a serene backdrop for leisurely activities such as fishing, birdwatching, and boat rides. The tranquil ambiance and scenic beauty of Deduru Oya attract both locals and tourists seeking respite from the hustle and bustle of urban life. Whether you're exploring its shores or simply soaking in the natural splendor, Deduru Oya offers a peaceful retreat amidst Sri Lanka's breathtaking landscapes.
